About Restaurant

Welcome to Baba Fareed Burger Point! We are a culinary destination where every bite is a passport to flavors of amore. Our restaurant offers a delightful dining experience with a wide range of delicious burgers and other mouthwatering dishes. Our team of talented chefs prepares each meal with love and attention to detail, ensuring that every dish is a culinary masterpiece. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a memorable dining experience.

Baba Fareed Burger Point has a rich history that dates back to 1985. It all started with a small food cart in the bustling streets of Lahore, Pakistan. The founder, Mr. Fareed, had a passion for creating delicious burgers that would leave a lasting impression on people's taste buds.
